PORTFOLIO 04 · MODULAR MASS

MODULAR MASS / CONTROLLED FORCEA STUDY OF MASS AND STRUCTURE

该项目研究在严格约束下，体量、结构与重复如何共存。 This project explores how mass, structure, and repetition coexist under strict control. 对象保持不变，变化只来自力、空间、材料与系统逻辑。 The object stays unchanged; only force, space, material, and system logic are allowed to shift.

ROLE · Product / Visual Designer TYPE · Structural Study YEAR · 25

Concept

Fixed Object · Shifting Logics

Single Object

椅子保持不变，允许变化的只有体量、力与场景。 The chair remains unchanged. Only mass, force, and context are allowed to shift.

Refusal of Variation

拒绝柔性与选择，对象因此暴露自身边界。 Flexibility is rejected. Choice is removed. The object reveals its limits.

Controlled System

重复、材料与空间逻辑取代叙事。 Repetition, material, and spatial logic replace narrative.

Method

One Object · One Geometry

Strict Rule

一个对象，一种几何，不再改造。 One object. One geometry. No redesign.

Allowed Shifts

只允许场景、光线、重复与分析绘图系统变化。 Spatial context, light behavior, repetition, and analytical drawing systems.

Structural Investigation

一致性让形式成为压力测试工具。 Consistency turns form into an instrument of pressure.

System & Repetition

From Object to Unit

Modeling Studies

Mass · Geometry · Consistency

Mass & Structure

Absorption · Constraint

Compressed Mass

软包体量吸收光线，抵抗分节。 The upholstered body absorbs light and resists articulation.

Structural Discipline

金属框架冷、连续且不可妥协。 Metal frame stays cold, continuous, and non-negotiable.

Oppositional Pair

体量对约束，吸收对反射，重量对连续性。 Mass vs constraint, absorption vs reflection, weight vs continuity.

Drawing as Proof

Force · Density · Time

Drawing as Pressure

线条延展、断裂、叠加，记录力的流向、体量聚集、空间占用、模块秩序与时间一致性。 Lines extend, break, overlap, and accumulate to record force flow, mass concentration, spatial occupation, modular order, and temporal consistency.

Material Logic

Behavior over Surface

Dense Textile

材质密实、消光、反应缓慢。 Matte, absorptive, and slow to respond.

Reflective Metal

金属具张力、连续且受控。 Tensile, continuous, and disciplined.

Structural Truth

材料决定行为，不是装饰。 Material decisions shape behavior, not decoration.
