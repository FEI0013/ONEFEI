PORTFOLIO 03 · THE INNER CUT

THE INNER CUTWhere precision meets intimacy.

THE INNER CUT 关注亲密、边界与控制。戒指作为“切割关系”的媒介，贴近身体却保留锋利距离。 THE INNER CUT explores intimacy, boundary, and control. The ring cuts relations—close to the body yet sharply distant. 绿色宝石像深水般的情绪密度，安静、缓慢，却不可忽视。 The green stone carries a deep-water density: quiet, slow, and impossible to ignore.

ROLE · Product / Visual Designer TYPE · Visual System YEAR · 2024

Core Concept

01 · Intimate Distance

建筑感 × 珠宝雕塑取向，强调遮挡（Occlusion）的视觉权力：帽檐遮住眼睛，只露出嘴唇、手、戒指；权力来自“隐藏”，而非“展示”。 Architectural jewelry sculpture focused on occlusion: the brim hides the eyes, leaving lips, hand, and ring. Power comes from hiding, not display.

Intimate Distance

在亲密中建立秩序，允许锋利靠近却不被吞没。 Order inside intimacy—sharpness is allowed near, never swallowed.

亲密是一种结构，而非浪漫装饰。 Intimacy is a structure, not decoration.

Inner Cut

不是装饰，而是“切割关系”的媒介：靠近、克制、留有切口。 Not ornament—an instrument that cuts relations: closeness, restraint, a kept incision.

Gemstone Depth

绿宝石不是自然明亮，而是深水般的情绪密度。 The green stone isn't bright nature, but deep-water density.

Curatorial Notes

02 · Precision & Tension

Multi-angle Reading

多角度与分构图不是展示，而是对同一对象的判断拆解。 Multi-angle and split frames dissect a single object, not merely display it.

Controlled Refraction

折射、气泡与深绿调构成被克制的危险感。 Refraction, bubbles, and deep green tones create restrained danger.

Keywords

精确 · 受控张力 · 静默危险 · 亲密距离 Precision · Controlled tension · Quiet danger · Intimate distance

Author Stance

03 · Psychological Relation

Not Ornament

我不以宝石价值或装饰性来定义这枚戒指。 I don't define the ring by gem value or ornament.

Relation Design

真正被设计的是佩戴者与物件之间的心理关系。 What is designed is the psychological relation between wearer and object.

Quiet Tension

当锋利被允许贴近身体，亲密本身成为结构。 When sharpness is allowed near the body, intimacy becomes structure.

Visual System

04 · 画面结构

Analysis

05 · 结构与材质

颜色与金属

宝石与金属的色阶控制统一在橄榄 / 军绿序列中，确保戒指在画面里是唯一“异物”。 Gem and metal tones stay within olive/army green so the ring reads as the only anomaly.

Short Version

06 · Project Summary

THE INNER CUT——一项关于亲密、精确与受控贴近张力的戒指项目。 THE INNER CUT — A ring project exploring intimacy, precision, and the quiet tension of controlled closeness.
