PORTFOLIO 01 · NIGHTFALL

NIGHTFALLPOWER · SILENCE · STRATEGY

Black Fragrance Concept。以棋盘的理性决策与剃须刀的锋利风险为核心隐喻，构建“可控秩序中允许危险存在”的黑色香水系统。香味不是即时吸引，而是博弈完成后的残留结果——稳定、贴肤、持续存在。 A black fragrance concept built on chessboard logic and razor risk. Control allows danger to exist; scent appears as the residue after the game—stable, skin-close, and persistent.

ROLE · Product / Visual Designer TYPE · Visual System YEAR · 25

PLAN A · 结构理性 · 冷静博弈

BLACK FRAGRANCE CONCEPT

结构隐喻

以棋盘与刀刃替代具象叙事，把秩序与风险变成结构语言。 Replace literal narrative with chessboard and blade, turning order and risk into structure.

控制本身，是最高级的表达。 Control itself is the highest expression.

极端克制

黑色视觉系统压低信息量，让“稳定存在感”成为主叙事。 A restrained black system reduces information so stable presence becomes the narrative.

秩序构图

强秩序构图与低信息画面，强调理性决策与结构化思维。 Ordered compositions and low-info frames emphasize rational decision and structure.

PLAN B · 危险亲密 · 私密仪式

BLACK FRAGRANCE CONCEPT

危险亲密

剃须是刀刃贴近喉咙的日常行为，危险却极度私人。 Shaving brings a blade to the throat—dangerous yet deeply private.

克制仪式

在理性计算中接近风险，在锋利边缘保持优雅。 Approach risk with calculation and keep elegance at the edge.

低声存在

香水贴肤工作，不宣告存在；动作结束后才被感知。 Scent works close to skin, never announcing itself—felt after the act.

不主动进攻，但从不失手。 It never attacks, but never misses.
